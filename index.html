About Me

My name is Yi Zhu, and I am a PhD candidate in Signal and Image Processing at the CREATIS Laboratory, University of Lyon, France.

My research focuses on digital twin modeling in dentistry through the fusion of CBCT (Cone Beam CT) and IOS (Intraoral Scan) data. I develop deep learning methods to combine low-resolution volumetric data with high-resolution surface scans.
